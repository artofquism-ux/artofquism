Communication

Communication Between the Conscious and the Unconscious Mind: The communication between the conscious and the unconscious mind gives birth to an inner language — reflected through numbers and alphabets in the Zero Hour of creation; the alphabetical sequence A—Z unfolds like a circle emerging from a single point, where 26 → 2 + 6 = 8 symbolizes the reflection of '0', the sign of an eternal cycle; just as the solar system extends from 0 to 8, embodying infinity, so do the numbers 0-9 represent the silent origin of human thought; dreams bridge the unconscious with the conscious, linking the inner and outer universes through the hidden pulse of awareness;

The birth of a newborn is reflected through the vibrations of light, heat, and sound — three primal forces that contain the essence of creation itself; the vowels a, e, i, o, u symbolize these sources of energy, embodying the natural rhythm hidden within every sound; just as birth arises from a dual source, the origin of sound too manifests through union — as the letters Q and U are always pronounced together; the sequence QRSTU thus represents the continuity of life, forming a subtle link between present existence and Earth's primordial origin;

The QRSTU series signifies the source of light, while VWXYZ embodies the source of heat; within this continuum, VW reflects the dual illumination of sunlight and moonlight, symbolizing the equilibrium between two luminous energies; the XY chromosomes reveal the biological essence of life's formation, whereas Z stands as a symbol of the "Zero Hour" — the cosmic womb, the moment where creation, time, and consciousness converge into the living form;

Thus, the sequence A—Z becomes a reflection of Life and Nature, where A—M and N—Z embody the cyclic rhythm of millennia; ABCD = WXYZ forms a symbolic circle — AD (Anno Domini) and BC (Before Christ), the two ends of time's continuum; WZ, meaning Word Zero, represents the figures from zero to nine — the "Zero Hour" or the cosmic womb of creation; from this alphabetic consciousness, XY chromosomes emerge, completing the cycle of self-realization — the union of creation, consciousness, and life itself;
