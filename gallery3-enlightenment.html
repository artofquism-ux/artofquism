Creation

Creation marks the origin of form — where silence gathers into image and imagination takes its first breath.

The beginning of life: The importance of p'water' at the beginning of life is immense—it plays a unique role in the formation of organic matter at the origin of creation; the process of evaporation in space in the representation of the solar system, which causes the regeneration of water through the rain cycle; in this process, the earth becomes a worthy vessel for life, where life is transmitted; in the spectrum of biodiversity, human life is reflected in the universal laws of nature; the cerebellum (as the flower of morning glory) is located in the center of the human spine—a complex and sensitive part—which not only acts as a physical balance, but also as a symbol of the unconscious mind, where the flow of human dreams and unconscious thoughts is reflected; the life cycle—birth, survival and death—which symbolizes an infinite cycle, life is therefore not only biological, but also an expression of a complete consciousness, here water, light, emotion, and dreams combine to form a unique creative resource, connected to nature and existence;
